📖 How to Use This Tool

Whether you're new to Meta Horizon+ or just want a better way to browse the full catalog, this tool was designed to help you figure out which games you actually want to try — and cut through the noise on the ones you don't.

⭐ Star a Game

Tap the star on any game to add it to your personal Starred Games List at the top of the page. Starred games show a larger gold star with no button background — easy to spot at a glance. Your stars are saved locally so they'll be there next time you open the tool.

To unstar a game, tap the star again from anywhere in the catalog, or tap the ✕ directly inside the Starred Games List — this removes it from the list without hiding it from the catalog.

Reordering: Inside the Starred Games List you have two ways to rearrange cards:

  • ↑ / ↓ buttons — tap ↑ to instantly move a card to the top of your list, or ↓ to send it to the bottom. An Undo toast appears briefly so you can reverse the move if needed.
  • PC / Mouse — click and drag anywhere on the card (outside of buttons) to move it to any position.
  • Mobile / Touch — use the ⋮⋮ grip handle (visible on wider screens) to drag, or use the ↑ / ↓ buttons for quick top/bottom jumps.

Your order is saved automatically and persists between sessions.

✕ Hide a Game

Already beaten it? Know it's not your thing? Hit the ✕ to hide it. This doesn't delete anything permanently — a brief Undo popup appears at the bottom so you can bring it right back. Hidden games are collected in the Hidden Games panel at the bottom of the page, shown with full card art and a red tint. Tap ↩ Restore on any card to add it back to the catalog.

🛒 Store Button

Opens the game's page directly on the Meta Horizon Store so you can read more, check reviews, or claim it.

On PC — opens the store website in a new browser tab, no issues.

On mobile — tapping Store will try to deep-link into the Meta Horizon app. If the app isn't already open, it may land on the app's main page instead of the game. Simply go back, make sure the Meta Horizon app is running in the background, then tap Store again — it'll go straight to the right page the second time.

🔍 Filters & Sorting

Main / Indie — toggle between catalog tiers Mar / Jan — filter by when games were added. Both can be active at once 👥 Multiplayer / 🤝 Co-op — filter by play style Not Starred — find games you haven't evaluated yet Genre dropdown — narrow by category A→Z / Z→A — sort alphabetically ⭐ Top Rated — sorts by rating adjusted for review count, so a 4.8 with 10,000 reviews ranks higher than a 4.8 with 12 reviews Rating filter (4.5+ to 4.9+) — show only games at or above a minimum rating

All filters stack — combine Main + Jan + Co-op + 4.7+ to drill down to exactly what you're looking for. When nothing matches, a message shows which filters are active with a one-tap reset.

★ Ratings and review counts are a snapshot from the last time this tool was updated and may not reflect the current Meta store.

☰ Options Menu

Top-left corner. From here you can:

  • View / Restore — open the Hidden Games panel to restore individual games
  • Restore All — bring all hidden games back to the catalog at once (asks for confirmation)
  • Clear Stars — remove all starred games and start fresh (asks for confirmation)
  • ✏️ Name List — give your list a custom name shown under the page title and included when you share
  • 📤 Export My List — share your starred and hidden games as a short link. Recipients open it and see your exact list. On mobile this opens the share sheet directly
  • 🔍 Filters — tap to expand a full filter panel without scrolling back to the top

📋 Navigation Bar

The four buttons at the top of the screen are quick-jump shortcuts:

  • ⭐ — jump to your Starred Games List. Dims when empty
  • 📋 — jump to the main catalog
  • 🗑 — jump to the Hidden Games panel. Dims when nothing is hidden
  • ? — open this help guide

All buttons auto-expand their section if it was collapsed.

⛶ Fullscreen Mode

For a more immersive experience, tap the fullscreen button in the top-right corner to expand the catalog edge-to-edge. Tap it again to exit.
