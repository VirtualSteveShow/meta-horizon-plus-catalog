📖 How to Use This Tool

Whether you're new to Meta Horizon+ or just want a better way to browse the full catalog, this tool was designed to help you figure out which games you actually want to try — and cut through the noise on the ones you don't.

⭐ Star a Game

Tap the ⭐ on any game to add it to your personal Starred Games List at the top of the page. Use this for games you want to try, revisit, or keep track of. Your stars are saved locally so they'll still be there next time you open the tool.

To unstar a game, tap the ⭐ again from anywhere in the catalog, or tap the ✕ directly inside the Starred Games List — this removes it from the list without hiding it from the catalog.

Reordering: Inside the Starred Games List, each card has a ⋮⋮ grip handle on the left edge. Press and hold it to drag the card up or down into your preferred order. Your ranking is saved automatically and persists between sessions.

The ⭐ button in the top navigation bar jumps straight to your Starred Games List — it dims when your list is empty.

✕ Remove a Game

Already beaten it? Know it's not your thing? Hit the ✕ to remove it from the list. This doesn't delete anything permanently — removed games are collected in the Hidden Games panel where you can restore them anytime. A shorter list feels a lot less daunting.

🛒 Store Button

Opens the game's page directly on the Meta Horizon Store so you can read more, check reviews, or claim it.

On PC — opens the store website in a new browser tab, no issues.

On mobile — tapping Store will try to deep-link into the Meta Horizon app. If the app isn't already open, it may land on the app's main page instead of the game. Simply go back, make sure the Meta Horizon app is running in the background, then tap Store again — it'll go straight to the right page the second time.

🔍 Filters & Sorting

Main / Indie — toggle between catalog tiers New in March / January — recently added games 👥 Multiplayer / 🤝 Co-op — filter by play style Not Starred — find games you haven't evaluated yet Genre dropdown — narrow by category A→Z / Z→A — sort alphabetically ⭐ Top Rated — sorts by rating adjusted for review count, so a 4.8 with 10,000 reviews ranks higher than a 4.8 with 12 reviews Rating filter (4.5+ to 4.9+) — show only games at or above a minimum rating

★ Ratings and review counts are a snapshot from the last time this tool was updated and may not reflect the current Meta store.

☰ Options Menu

Top-left corner. From here you can view and restore any removed games, restore everything at once, or clear all your stars to start fresh.

It also has a 🔍 Filters section — tap it to expand a full set of filters and search without scrolling back to the top. Everything stays in sync with the main filter bar.

⛶ Fullscreen Mode

For a more immersive experience, tap the fullscreen button in the top-right corner to expand the catalog edge-to-edge. Tap it again to exit.
